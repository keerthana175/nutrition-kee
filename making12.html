Fruit Pizza share

This simple fruit pizza is beautiful and delicious! A soft sugar cookie crust with a cream cheese frosting and topped with sliced fruit. So simple and oh-so good!


Calories : 587 cal

Cook Time : 90 min

Diet Type : Veg

Meal Type :Breakfast

INGREDIENTS

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 small bunch of grapes, halved
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 mango, sliced
  • 5–6 kiwis, sliced
  • 7–10 strawberries, sliced
  • 2 Tbsp honey
  • 1 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 12 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened

    • INSTRUCTIONS

    1. Mix the butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla until well combined. (You can use an electric mixer or just mix with a spoon if your butter is melted.) Add flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix until combined. Chill the dough for 30 minutes.
    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Line a baking stone with parchment. Roll the chilled dough out onto it into a 1/4 to 1/2-inch thick layer, leaving some space around the edge. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Allow it to cool.
    3. Using an electric mixer, combine the cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar and vanilla until smooth and creamy. Spread over the cooled cookie crust and chill again to firm up the frosting.
    4. Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds and black pepper, if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!
    5. Top with fruit!

    NUTRIENTS
