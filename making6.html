Berry French Toast Casserole share

Berry French Toast Casserole is a delightful twist on the classic breakfast favorite. This mouthwatering dish combines the comforting flavors of French toast with the vibrant sweetness of fresh berries.


Calories : 345 Kcal

Cook Time : 45 min

Diet Type : Veg

Meal Type : Breakfast

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 loaf sourdough bread
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk of choice
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen berries
  • 4 Tbsp cream cheese
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup

    • INSTRUCTIONS

    1. In a 9×13 baking dish coated with nonstick spray, arrange bread in an even layer and set aside.
    2. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, Greek yogurt, maple syrup, cinnamon and vanilla, mixing well, then pour the egg mixture over top of the bread, making sure each piece is nicely coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, but best overnight.
    3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and remove casserole from fridge while the oven preheats.
    4. Keep the casserole covered with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 to 25 minutes, until the casserole is set and the top is nice and golden.
    5. While the casserole bakes you can make the cream cheese drizzle. In a small bowl, mix together cream cheese, maple syrup and milk using a hand mixer (or whisk) until cream cheese has a drizzle consistency. Feel free to add more (or less) milk as needed!
    6. Remove casserole from the oven and top with an assortment of berries and drizzle with the cream cheese mixture. If you’re not a fan of cream cheese, feel free to top with additional maple syrup or powdered sugar. Serve and enjoy!
